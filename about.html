ABOUT THE STORE

Geek Store TN is a shop where you can find a lot of geeky items such as accessories , stickers , mugs , clothes , and many other rare products related to anime , series , movies and gaming.

OUR STORY

Geek Store TN has been started as an idea , as a need for geeky and unique products. The goal was to create those items for ourselves seeking originality , but people kept asking about our secret source so we decided to share our products with them . And that’s how we made this store and that’s how we will continue working on it
